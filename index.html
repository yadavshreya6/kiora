About Kaiora

Born from the quiet ingenuity of a family workshop high in the Himalayan foothills, Kaiora weaves together three generations of heartfelt craftsmanship into every creation. As India's first homegrown luxury house uniting precision wristwatches, innovative ring‑watches, and evocative perfumes, we honor the hands that shaped our heritage and the stones, silks, and scents that tell our story.


Each timepiece, fragrance, and ring‑watch carries the soul of those early artisans—bearing witness to a journey of humble beginnings, subtle refinements, and bold new horizons. More than accessories, our collections serve as vessels of authentic emotion: reminders of shared memories, markers of personal milestones, and tokens of timeless connection.
